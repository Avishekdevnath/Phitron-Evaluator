## Pro Tips for AI Generators

### Midjourney Syntax
```
/imagine prompt: [description] --size 512 --style raw --niji 6
```

### DALL-E Best Practices
- Be specific about colors (use hex codes)
- Mention "flat design" or "minimal" for style
- Specify size (24x24, 32x32, etc.)
- Say "no text, icon only"
- Use "solid filled" for clarity
- Mention "white background" for consistency

### General Tips
1. **Be Specific**: Use exact color codes
2. **Mention Style**: "flat design", "minimal", "modern"
3. **Set Size**: "24x24 pixels" or "32x32"
4. **No Text**: Say "icon only, no labels"
5. **Background**: Specify white or transparent
6. **Purpose**: Briefly describe what it represents
